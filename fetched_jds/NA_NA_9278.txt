About EY Px
EYPx is a multi-disciplinary analytics team under the EY Parthenon group which is part of Strategy
and Transactions service line. EYPx focuses on delivering client projects and solutions across
Data Management, Data Science, AI, Advanced Analytics, Visualization, Business Analytics and
Automation. The assignments cover a wide range of countries and industry sectors.
The opportunity
We’re looking for an employee with knowledge in data science, strong understanding in Machine
Learning; Python Programming and responsible for developing solutions using data driven
techniques and Machine learning models.

and develop models and solutions that deliver value to the business.

to automate processes, recommend actions and help in enhancing value creation opportunities on
transactions.
• Development and validation of predictive models using machine learning algorithms.
• Deliver value through insights, reporting and data visualization techniques.
• Cross collaborate across functions and regional teams to design and build analytics solutions.
Skills and attributes for success

Science, ML, LLM, NLP, Deep learning, Scikit-learn, TensorFlow, or similar.
• Understanding of Software development principle
• Strong analytical and problem-solving skills with attention to detail.
• Good interpersonal skills and excellent written and verbal communication skill
• Team player, open, pleasure to work with and positive in a group dynamic.
