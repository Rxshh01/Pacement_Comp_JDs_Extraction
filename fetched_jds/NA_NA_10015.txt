Job Details
Key Responsibilities:
- Assist in the development and testing of firmware for biometric sensors to ensure high performance and reliability.
- Collaborate with hardware engineers to integrate and enhance sensor performance.
- Participate in the testing and validation of firmware to maintain product quality.
- Help identify and troubleshoot firmware issues to ensure smooth product functionality.
- Contribute to the documentation of firmware development processes and testing outcomes.
Requirements:
- A bachelor's degree in Electronics, Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, or a related field.
- Solid understanding of C/C++ programming and a willingness to develop problem-solving skills.
- Basic knowledge of communication protocols such as I2C, SPI, and BLE, with an interest in gaining hands-on experience.
- Familiarity with ARM Cortex M4 and M3 microcontrollers (coursework or projects).
- Foundational knowledge of debugging tools such as oscilloscopes, logic analyzers, or JTAG.
- Understanding of Real-Time Operating Systems (RTOS) (academic or project-based experience).
- Familiarity with FLASH memory device drivers and low power device concepts is a plus.
- Willingness to learn and work with embedded debuggers and quality control tools.
- Interest or prior project experience related to firmware development for sensors (biometric or otherwise).
Nice to Have:
- Coursework or projects involving additional sensor integration.
- Knowledge of power management techniques.
