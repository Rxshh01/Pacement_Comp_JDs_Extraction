Tritorc is a front runner EPC Company with a global client base ranging from the Americas to the Far East with

designing and developing tools and equipment for the MRO industry. A leader in the bolting and onsite machining
industry, Tritorc with a three-decade legacy of transformative growth and innovations, now seeks to step up to the
next level. If meticulous preparation and attention to detail with data- driven decisions are your forte, then this

benchmark.
Key Responsibilities:
You are required to display the following traits:
Strategic Leadership:
Develop and execute the company's R&D strategy, aligning it with the organization's overall
business goals and objectives.
We require you to Build the A-Team that shall deliver the vision you shall build.
Additionally, the R&D Engineer shall
- Establish project goals and priorities by collaborating with Marketing and Operations.
- Transfer new technologies, products, and manufacturing processes into and out -the company.

All of these activities shall be focussed on delivering an experience to the customer that is based on understanding
