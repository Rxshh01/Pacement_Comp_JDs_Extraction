Position Summary
】
Our team is building a wide range of natural language
processing technologies intended to be applied to entertainment content
creation and business data analysis.
You will work on advanced research in natural language processing. Your main
task will be the research and development of multilingual LLM and downstream
task for content creation intended to reduce hurdles for content distribution
and make workflow more efficient.
We expect to utilize this technology in movies, anime and games and contribute
to deliver Sony's content to more customers faster.

collaborative research with overseas laboratories and/or business groups, data
collection, library releases, patent proposals, improving efficiency in Sony`s
business workflow, etc.
