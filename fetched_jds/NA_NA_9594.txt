Job Details
As a
Product Analyst
, you will play a pivotal role in analyzing data, supporting product teams, and helping drive product strategy through insights. You will work closely with cross-functional teams to collect and interpret data, identify market opportunities, and assist in building exceptional user experiences. This role is ideal for someone eager to dive into the product lifecycle and contribute from day one, with a focus on data-driven decision-making.
Key Responsibilities:
Data Analysis & Reporting
:
Collect, analyze, and interpret data to assess product performance and areas for improvement.
Assist in building dashboards and reports using tools like Excel, SQL, Python, and Google Analytics.
Monitor and track product key performance indicators (KPIs) and help derive actionable insights.
Market Research & User Insights
:
Conduct research on market trends, competitive landscape, and emerging opportunities.
Gather user feedback and preferences through data analysis to support product strategy and development.
Stay informed on industry trends, best practices, and competitor products.
Support Product Development
:
Collaborate with product managers to understand product requirements and help define user stories and specifications.
Participate in product testing and validation to ensure quality before launch.
Assist in the creation of product documentation and user flows.
Project Coordination
:
Support project management by tracking tasks, managing timelines, and coordinating with stakeholders.
Assist in managing product lifecycle phases, from concept to launch.
Communication & Cross-functional Collaboration
:
Present data insights and findings to stakeholders, including product managers and engineers.
Work with teams across design, engineering, and marketing to align on product goals and execution strategies.
