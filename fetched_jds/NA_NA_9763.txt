Job Details
Associate Consultant – Gen AI
The opportunity
In  this  role  you  would  be  required  to  be  inquisitive,  analytical,  ambitious  and  great  at  working with
people. You'll need to be a good team player and demonstrate the potential to become a  future business
leader. You'll need to be prepared to challenge us, to act on your own initiative  and to make an impact on
our teams and clients from day one.
Your key responsibilities
• Project Analysis: Support in-depth analysis of generative AI projects by understanding objectives,
requirements, and projected impacts on the client’s business.
• Data Preparation: Collect, clean, and organize data for AI model training, ensuring data quality,
consistency, and compliance with ethical standards.
• Algorithmic Exploration: Gain a foundational understanding of generative AI algorithms to provide
input on model optimization and enhancements.
• Model Evaluation: Execute model validation techniques to assess AI model accuracy, effectiveness,
and suitability across different scenarios.
• Cross-functional Collaboration: Work closely with data scientists, engineers, and domain experts to
contribute to integrating AI solutions into broader business strategies.
• Ethical AI Practices: Apply ethical AI principles by considering data privacy, fairness, and bias
mitigation throughout the model development lifecycle.
• Learning and Development: Keep up-to-date with advancements in generative AI and data science
methodologies to bring innovative approaches to projects.
Tools and Techniques:
• Programming Languages: Basic proficiency in Python, Pyspark or R for data analysis, scripting, and
initial model development.
• Machine Learning Frameworks: Exposure to ML frameworks such as TensorFlow or PyTorch, especially
for generative AI models, is preferred.
• Generative AI Models: Familiarity with using APIs of publicly available generative AI models like GPT-
3.5/4, DALL-E, and Llama.
• Data Visualization: Competence in visualization tools like Matplotlib, Seaborn, or Tableau to
communicate insights clearly.
• Statistical Analysis: Foundational understanding of statistical methods for data analysis and hypothesis
testing.
• Version Control: Exposure to version control tools, preferably Git, to track project progress and
collaborate with teammates effectively.
• Cloud Platforms: Knowledge of cloud services (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud) for data storage and basic
model training purposes.
• NLP Techniques: Basic understanding of Natural Language Processing (NLP) libraries such as NLTK and
spaCy for text-based projects.
• Application Development: Exposure to quick prototyping tools like Gradio or Streamlit for developing
data application demos.
To qualify for the role, you must have
• Education: Degree in a quantitative field (e.g., Engineering, Economics, Statistics, Data Science) with a
strong academic record.
• Technical Skills: Familiarity with data science tools, big data platforms, and any hands-on project
experience is a plus.
• Communication Skills: Strong written and verbal communication abilities to present insights to
technical and non-technical stakeholders.
• Mindset: Analytical thinker with a passion for data science, adaptable, collaborative, and eager to
learn and grow.
• Soft Skills: Ability to work effectively in a team, solve complex problems, and maintain a proactive
approach to professional development.
Good to have
• Experience with data visualization or app development tools.
• Some exposure to working with big data platforms (e.g., Hadoop, Spark).
• Knowledge of client interaction and presentation skills.
What we look for
People with the ability to work in a collaborative way to provide services across multiple client
departments while adhering to commercial and legal requirements. You will need a practical
approach to solving issues and complex problems with the ability to deliver insightful and
practical solutions.
