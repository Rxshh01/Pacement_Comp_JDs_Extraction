Mtech Electrical JD
Job Description
• Assist in the design, development, and testing of electrical systems, components, and products.
• Collaborate with project teams to understand electrical requirements, specifications, and goals.
• Create and interpret electrical schematics, wiring diagrams, and layouts.
• Perform basic calculations for electrical load, voltage, current, and circuit design.
• Participate in troubleshooting and resolving issues in electrical systems or products.
• Conduct testing, measurement, and data collection to ensure systems meet performance standards.
• Document test results, design modifications, and project progress for technical reports.
• Use electrical design software and tools (e.g., AutoCAD Electrical, MATLAB, and PCB design tools) to support project tasks.
• Assist in preparing cost estimates, schedules, and material lists for electrical projects.
• Ensure compliance with safety standards, industry codes, and regulatory requirements.
Skills & Competencies
• Basic knowledge of electrical engineering principles, circuit design, and electrical codes.
• Familiarity with electrical design and simulation software (e.g., AutoCAD, MATLAB, PSpice) is a plus.
• Understanding of industry-specific standards, such as IEEE and NEC.
• Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
• Attention to detail with the ability to follow instructions and complete tasks accurately.
• Good communication skills and the ability to work effectively in a team setting.
Education Required
• Bachelor’s and Master’s degree in Electrical Engineering or a related field.
