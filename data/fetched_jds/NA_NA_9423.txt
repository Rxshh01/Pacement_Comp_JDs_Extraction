Who are we and what do we do?
BrowserStack is the world's leading software testing platform powering over two million tests
every day across 15 global data centers. BrowserStack's products help developers build bug-free
software for the 5 billion internet users accessing websites and mobile applications through
millions of combinations of digital environments—devices, browsers, operating systems, and
versions. We help Tesco, Shell, NVIDIA, Discovery, Wells Fargo, and over 50,000 customers deliver
quality software at speed by moving testing to our Cloud. With BrowserStack, Dev and QA teams
can move fast while delivering an amazing experience for every customer.
BrowserStack was founded by Ritesh Arora and Nakul Aggarwal in 2011 with the vision of
becoming the testing infrastructure for the internet. We recently secured $200 million in Series B
funding at a $4 billion valuation in June 2021.
