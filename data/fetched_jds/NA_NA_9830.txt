Job Details

learning algorithms, and interactive game development to deliver state-of-the-art AI-driven 
applications for the energy and manufacturing industries. We are seeking a versatile and 
innovative Software, Deep Learning, and Game Development Engineer to join our dynamic 
team. In this multifaceted role, you will develop sophisticated software systems, implement 
cutting-edge deep learning models, and create immersive simulations and visualizations that 
enhance our energy forecasting, fault prediction, and digital twin technologies
Key Responsibilities  -
1.   Design, develop, and maintain robust software applications 
tailored to our AI-driven solutions
2. Implement scalable and efficient code using modern 
programming languages and frameworks
3. Develop, train, and optimize deep learning models for 
applications such as energy forecasting, fault prediction, and 
anomaly detection
4. Create interactive simulations and visualizations using game 
development platforms (e.g., Unity, Unreal Engine) to 
represent complex energy systems and industrial operations.
5. Ensure seamless integration of software, deep learning 
models, and simulation tools into our existing infrastructure.
Qualifications -
1. Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software 
Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, Game Development, or a 
related field. A Master’s degree is preferred.
2. Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, C++, 
C#, or JavaScript.
3. Familiarity with data preprocessing, feature engineering, and 
model evaluation techniques.
4. Experience with game development platforms (e.g., Unity, 
Unreal Engine) and creating interactive simulations.
5. Knowledge of cloud platforms (e.g., AWS, Azure, Google 
Cloud) and deployment strategies for scalable applications
