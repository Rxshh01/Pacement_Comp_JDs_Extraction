Business Overview:
AXP Finance organization drives performance while upholding the company's commitment to regulatory compliance and financial integrity through strategic collaboration with internal business partners and external constituents including the investment community and regulators. By demonstrating its unique view that spans across the Blue Box, Finance supports innovation and transformation by helping identify and prioritize the best and most impactful growth opportunities for the company.
The Data Analytics functions within Finance partner across the enterprise to drive analyses and forecasts for the key metrics and investment decisions across the enterprise. The team demonstrates the latest analytics techniques to provide insights on both current and future performance. Our mission is to transform the way analytics credibly drives sustainable, long-term growth for the enterprise, while also being early adopters of next-generation technology. We re-invent forecasting, tracking and analytical processes, demonstrating the latest tools and set the standard for advanced analytics.
