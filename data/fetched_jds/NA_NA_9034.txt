Position Summary
】
For this position, we are looking for researchers with
strong expertise in data science and machine learning, including causal
inference, recommender systems, sports analysis, or related fields. Candidates
should be capable of developing and evaluating such algorithms using real data
from commercial services.
Sony group aims to engage with over 1 billion users through its
direct-to-consumer (DTC) services. To achieve the goal as a research and
development hub for the einter group companies, our team upholds the theme of
"Human-Centered Data Science" and conducts research and development
within the following scope:
Human as a Data Source:
- Acquiring customers and enhancing user engagement through personalization
- Understanding behaviors and extracting insights both online and offline
- Creating new experiences based on these technologies and insights
Human as a Data User:
- Developing AI and data science technologies that are user-friendly for data
analysts and creators
- Enhancing interaction technologies for improved usability and effectiveness
